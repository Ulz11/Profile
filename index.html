Reading Journey

"Mathematics for Human Flourishing"

Mathematics for Human Flourishing

"Mathematics for Human Flourishing" is a profound and thought-provoking work that redefines the role of mathematics in our lives. It argues that math is not just about numbers or equations, but a deeply human pursuit that cultivates virtues like curiosity, creativity, and perseverance. Through personal stories and philosophical reflections, Francis Su highlights how mathematics can address our deepest desires for truth, beauty, and justice, while also fostering community and resilience. The book transcends technical boundaries, offering a vision of math as a means to understand the world and flourish as individuals.

"Financial System 2030"

Financial System 2030

The financial system is currently confronted with tremendous challenges from the global economy, trade, politics, demographics, and most recently from enormous technological advancements. These developments have the capacity to change the existing financial system fundamentally. This book addresses how technological developments and digitalization will impact the future of financial systems.

"The System of the World"

The System of the World

The System of the World by Pierre-Simon Laplace is a key work that presents the laws of celestial mechanics in a way that is both scientifically rigorous and accessible to a general audience. By explaining the structure of the solar system, the forces at play in the universe, and offering a deterministic view of reality, Laplace established himself as one of the foremost scientists of the Enlightenment era. His work laid the groundwork for much of modern astrophysics and remains a monumental achievement in the history of science.
